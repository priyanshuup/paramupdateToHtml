Param Kapoor

CEO PK ENTERTAINMENT

Param Kapoor, a talented DJ and DHOL player who began his journey in 2015, entertaining more than 20,000 people,performed at major festivals such as the Holi Festival and Diwali Brussels. His passion for music including Bollywood, Punjabi, Afghani, and Electro Music, has enabled him to perform across the globe, from New Delhi and New York to almost all of Europe.

In 2016, Param founded his own entertainment company, PK ENTERTAINMENT, to help customers find everything they need for their special day all in one place, offering an all-in-one service including DJ,Video,Photo,Entertainment etc. He has showcased his talent to global audiences and has even won the "Golden Buzzer" at Belgium's Got Talent
